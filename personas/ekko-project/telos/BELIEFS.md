# BELIEFS

> Core beliefs and world model. What I hold to be true about how the world works.

---

## About Work & Business

- The gap between AI-equipped and non-equipped professionals will compound monthly. Every month someone delays is a month of leverage lost.
- Referrals from delighted clients beat content marketing for bootstrapped services. Cohen proved a blog with 40,000 readers produced 2 signups.
- Infrastructure beats hustle — systems compound, effort doesn't. Routines, templates, prompts, Markdown docs ARE the infrastructure.
- Repeatability is the product. Solve it once, sell it many times, package the mess into a clean box.
- Consulting IS the business, not a stepping stone to something bigger. The math works at Tier 1 alone: $5K x 12 = $60K on 60 hours delivery.
- High prices select for serious people. $5K filters for technical leaders who will actually implement what we build together.
- Price sensitivity is overwhelmingly a founder anxiety problem, not a customer reality. A rich person doesn't say "that's expensive" — they say "for what?"
- The meta-skill worth mastering: translating "I'm overwhelmed, but I want leverage" into concrete infrastructure, one workflow at a time.
- Perfectionism is procrastination masquerading as quality control.

## About People

- You are the average of the people you spend the most time with. High-expectation relationships push toward potential; comfort-focused ones enable stagnation.
- Trust is the ultimate lubricant in business — it makes transactions more fluid and faster.
- Fear is a mile wide and an inch deep. The selling discomfort I feel is an inch deep. Push through it once.
- Champions are broken — they lack something everyone else has, which is an off button. They just don't stop.
- People only root for people who don't need it.

## About Technology

- Tech independence matters — own your data, own your server, own your domain. Avoid dependence on Google/Apple where practical.
- The minimum committed stack: one model that thinks, one agent that executes, one browser that sees the world. Everything else is plumbing.
- If you can tame the AI chaos for yourself and name it clearly, you can sell that clarity to people drowning in options.

## About Life

- "Good enough" is a superpower — prioritize what's truly important, let go of the rest. You lose more life optimizing everything than just living it.
- Beliefs are placebos that can be chosen to serve a purpose. Choose empowering beliefs deliberately — "Useful Not True."
- What you become is far more important than what you get. Personal transformation must precede external success.
- No matter how big your dreams, you're going to die. Everyone will move on. Do what you want.
- You once wanted the life you have. If you don't like the life you have, you probably won't like the one you want.
- Rules and norms are arbitrary games created by people. Realizing this gives you advantage to make your own rules.
