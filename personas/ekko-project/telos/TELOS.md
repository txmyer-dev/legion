# TELOS — Tony's Life Operating System

> The central hub for everything I'm building toward. Not a productivity system — a life system.

## What This Is

TELOS is where I centralize what I want from **life**, not just tasks. It's the thing that makes Ekko feel different from ChatGPT — because Ekko knows what I'm actually trying to achieve, not just what I typed today.

Every file in this directory is a facet of my life context. Together they form the operating system that Ekko uses to make better decisions on my behalf.

---

## The Core Insight

I'm not building a business. I'm building **infrastructure** — routines, templates, prompts, systems — and packaging that infrastructure for others who are drowning in options and just want "the stack that works."

The meta-skill I'm rehearsing: translating "I'm overwhelmed, but I want leverage" into concrete infrastructure, one workflow at a time.
